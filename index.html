About GCSS

More about us Government Comprehensive Secondary School (GCSS) is committed to delivering high-quality education to students in Opu-Nembe, Bassambiri, Bayelsa State. Established with the vision of fostering academic excellence and moral integrity, GCSS offers a well-rounded curriculum that prepares students for future success.
